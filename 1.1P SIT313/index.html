Project Name: ToDoApp

Project Description:

TodoApp is a user-friendly and efficient task management application designed to help individuals and teams stay organized and productive. It offers a simple yet powerful interface for creating, managing, and tracking tasks. Users can add new tasks, set deadlines, prioritize tasks, and mark them as complete. The app supports features such as task categorization, reminders, and progress tracking to ensure that users can easily keep track of their to-do lists and meet their goals. With TodoApp, staying on top of daily tasks and long-term projects has never been easier.
